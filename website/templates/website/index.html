Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?

Click "Sign Up" and follow the steps to create your account using your JCPS email or organization details.

Who can volunteer?

JCPS students with a valid school email can join as volunteers.

How are volunteer hours tracked?

Organizations verify your hours after completion, and you can view them in your dashboard.

Can organizations post opportunities?

Yes, verified organizations can post volunteer opportunities directly on the platform.

Is ServeSync free to use?

Yes, ServeSync is free for both students and organizations to join and use.
